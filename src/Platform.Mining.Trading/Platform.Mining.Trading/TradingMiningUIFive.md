Warehouse-Facing Enhancements

These expand the warehouse module to handle direct deliveries from producers.

A. Inbound Delivery Verification Screen

View pending deliveries from mines

Match to expected trade/lot IDs

Verify QC, weight, and documentation

Generate electronic Warehouse Receipt (WR)

Notify producer + buyer + clearing

B. Outbound / Allocation Screen

Track when WRs are transferred to buyers post-trade settlement

Generate dispatch orders for pickup or export

C. Inventory Ledger (by ownership)

Live list of all receipts, quantities, grades, and ownership status

Filter by producer, buyer, or warehouse location
